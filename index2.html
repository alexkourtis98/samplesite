  • What is R2 certification?

    R2 certification is a globally recognized standard for electronics recyclers, ensuring they meet strict environmental, health, and safety requirements.

  • Why is R2 certification important?

    R2 certification is important because it helps businesses comply with regulations, protect the environment, ensure data security, and enhance their reputation.

  • How can a business obtain R2 certification?

    To obtain R2 certification, a business must undergo an audit by an accredited certification body, demonstrating compliance with the R2 standard.

  • What are the benefits of R2 certification?

    R2 certification provides businesses with access to new markets, a competitive advantage, risk mitigation, and the ability to contribute to a sustainable circular economy.
